# Statistics & Inference

> Learn how to draw reliable conclusions from data using probability, sampling, hypothesis testing, confidence intervals, effect sizes, and statistical modeling. Statistics forms the mathematical foundation of Data Science, Machine Learning, A/B Testing, and scientific experimentation.

**Last Reviewed:** 2026-06

**Prerequisites:** [01 – Foundations / Probability & Statistics](../01-foundations/mathematics/probability-statistics.md) · [EDA & Visualization](eda-and-visualization.md)

---

>  **Tip:** Focus on understanding *why* statistical methods work instead of memorizing formulas. Visual intuition and hands-on experiments are far more valuable.
