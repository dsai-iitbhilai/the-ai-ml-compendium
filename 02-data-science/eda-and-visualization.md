# EDA & Visualization

> Explore data through statistics and visual storytelling. Learn to uncover patterns, detect anomalies, validate assumptions, and communicate insights using Python visualization libraries.

**Last Reviewed:** 2026-06

**Prerequisites:** [Data Wrangling](data-wrangling.md)

---

>  **Tip:** Great EDA isn't about making beautiful charts—it's about asking the right questions. Every visualization should answer a specific business or scientific question.
