# Must-Read Classics

> Foundational papers that shaped modern machine learning and deep learning.

## Resource Table

| Category | Title | Description | Level |
|---|---|---|---|
| 📄 Paper | [Attention Is All You Need — Vaswani et al. 2017](https://example.com/attention-paper) | The Transformer architecture; foundation of modern NLP/CV | Advanced |
| 📄 Paper | [ImageNet Classification with Deep CNNs — Krizhevsky et al. 2012](https://example.com/alexnet-paper) | AlexNet — the paper that started the deep learning revolution | Intermediate |
| 📄 Paper | [Deep Residual Learning for Image Recognition — He et al. 2016](https://example.com/resnet-paper) | ResNet — skip connections enabling very deep networks | Intermediate |
| 📄 Paper | [Generative Adversarial Nets — Goodfellow et al. 2014](https://example.com/gan-paper) | GANs — generative modeling via adversarial training | Advanced |
| 📄 Paper | [Dropout: A Simple Way to Prevent Neural Nets from Overfitting — Srivastava et al. 2014](https://example.com/dropout-paper) | Dropout regularization technique | Intermediate |
| 📄 Paper | [Random Forests — Breiman 2001](https://example.com/random-forests-paper) | Ensemble method that remains widely used today | Intermediate |

## Honorable Mentions

| Paper | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| [BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers](https://example.com/bert-paper) | Brought transfer learning to NLP; set new benchmarks |
| [Playing Atari with Deep RL — Mnih et al. 2013](https://example.com/dqn-paper) | DQN — deep reinforcement learning breakthrough |
| [Batch Normalization — Ioffe & Szegedy 2015](https://example.com/batchnorm-paper) | Stabilizes and accelerates deep network training |
| [Word2Vec — Mikolov et al. 2013](https://example.com/word2vec-paper) | Distributed word embeddings at scale |

## Recommended Reading Order for Beginners

1. **Random Forests** — accessible ensemble method
2. **Dropout** — simple idea with big impact
3. **AlexNet** — see how deep learning solved a hard problem
4. **ResNet** — understand the innovation that unlocked depth
5. **Attention Is All You Need** — the modern foundation
