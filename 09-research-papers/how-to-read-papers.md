## The Three-Pass Method (Keshav)

| Pass | Time | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| **1st Pass** | ~5 min | Get a high-level understanding (title, abstract, figures, conclusion) |
| **2nd Pass** | ~30 min | Understand the method and key results (ignore proofs/appendix) |
| **3rd Pass** | ~1-2 hrs | Deep understanding — reconstruct the paper, critique assumptions |

## Staying Current

- Subscribe to **arXiv RSS feeds** for specific categories (cs.LG, cs.CL, cs.CV)
- Follow **Papers with Code** weekly newsletters
- Use **Twitter/X** and **LinkedIn** to follow researchers and labs
- Join **journal clubs** (local or online) for discussion
