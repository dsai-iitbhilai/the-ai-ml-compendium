## Why Read Papers?

- Understand the **why** behind common techniques
- Stay current with **rapidly evolving fields** (GenAI, agents)
- Build **intuition** that tutorials and blogs often skip
- Prepare for **research roles** and **ML interviews**

## Suggested Reading Workflow

1. **Skim** — title, abstract, figures, conclusion (5 min)
2. **Read** — introduction + method + experiments (30 min)
3. **Critique** — what's missing? limitations? follow-ups? (15 min)
4. **Synthesize** — write a 1-paragraph summary in your own words
