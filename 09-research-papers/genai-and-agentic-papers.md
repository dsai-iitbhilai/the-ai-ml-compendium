## How to Approach These Papers

- Start with **RAG** and **ReAct** — accessible and highly practical for building applications today.
- Move to **Toolformer** and **LLaMA** — excellent for understanding architectural insights and API integration.
- Tackle the **GPT-4 Report** and **DDPM** — these require more technical depth and mathematical comfort.
- Follow up with **blog posts**, official **system cards**, and **implementation repositories** (like HuggingFace) to see the code behind the math.
