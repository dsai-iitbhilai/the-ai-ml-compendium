## Key Concepts Checklist

- Decision boundaries and how different models draw them
- Effect of learning rate, regularization, and activation functions
- Overfitting and underfitting (visible in real time)
- Impact of dataset size and separability on model performance
- Convolution kernels and feature maps (CNN Explainer)
- Gradient descent visualized (loss landscape exploration)
