## Why Join the Community?

- **Stay current** — ML moves fast; community keeps you updated
- **Get help** — faster answers than searching alone
- **Network** — job opportunities, collaborations, mentorship
- **Contribute** — open-source, blog posts, conference talks

## Community Etiquette

- **Search first** — most questions have been answered before
- **Be specific** — provide context, code, and error messages
- **Be respectful** — different experience levels are welcome
- **Share back** — when you solve something, write it up
